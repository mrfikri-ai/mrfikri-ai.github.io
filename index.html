About Me

Muhamad Rausyan Fikri was a lecturer and acting interim Head of Information Systems at Sampoerna University (SU), Indonesia. He served as a Global Lecturer for Systems and Industrial Engineering (SIE) and Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of Arizona (UA), USA, and was lab coordinator at the IoT Lab & Cyber-Physical Systems Research Group (CPS) at SU. He received a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Electronics from Universitas Gadjah Mada (UGM) in 2015, and an M.Eng. in Systems & Control Engineering from Tokyo Institute of Technology in 2018.
